## About me

I'm **Abraham Yin** (幽靈), studying **Computer Science** at **Brigham Young University–Idaho (BYU-Idaho)**. I like building software, tinkering with side projects, and spending time on board games, amateur radio (HAM), and whatever else catches my interest.

## A line I like

> Across the Great Wall, into the world.

It comes from the **first email sent from mainland China to the international internet**, on **20 September 1987**—successfully transmitted from Beijing to Germany, marking an early connection between China and the global network. To me, it also reads as an attitude: technology can cross barriers and link us to places farther away.
